Concept

What Bloc Mood is about

Made for the dark

Bloc Mood is built around the moment when the lights drop and the bass hits. Pieces are designed to live at night: black bases, clean silhouettes, details that glow or reflect under stage lights.

From the bloc to the stage

The “bloc” is your crew, your city, your corner. The “stage” is every place where the music gets loud — festivals, clubs, rooftops, beach parties. Bloc Mood connects both: everyday comfort with live-show energy.

Story

Who we are

Born in Morocco, built for the scene

Bloc Mood is an independent streetwear project shaped by nights between Casablanca, Rabat, Agadir and Essaouira. No big budgets or fake hype — just a real love for live music, design and the people in the crowd.

Limited runs, long stories

Every drop is small on purpose. We’d rather sell out than sit on piles of stock. Each piece is tied to a mood, a season, a city — something you’ll remember when you look at it years later.

Drop 01 · Glow

The first Bloc Mood drop focuses on one idea: pieces that stay quiet in daylight and turn on when the lights go down. Think black tees and hoodies with glowing lines, small florals or smileys that appear under UV and stage lights.

Line-up: oversized heavyweight tees, mid-weight hoodies and a hands-free crossbody bag for your phone, wallet, earplugs and one more thing.

It’s a small run. Once it’s gone, it becomes part of the story — no restock, just memories from that season.
